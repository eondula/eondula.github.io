A little about me

Background

My research interests are reinforcement learning, computational modeling and application of computational tools for societal impact. Prior to joining USC, I was a Research Software Engineer at IBM Research Africa. My focus then was research and development of mobile, web and Internet of Things applications to address challenges in the Agricultural, Water and Health Systems in the African context. I served as a member of the Distributed Ledgers and Artificial Intelligence Taskforce for the Government of Kenya and renders my expertise in advising tech start-ups across the African and Caribbean on AI and data-driven development.
