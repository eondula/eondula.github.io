Background

I spend a lot of time thinking, designing and building about artificial agents running on computational tools. These theoretical agents are based off reinforcement learning algorithms that operate on a closed bounded environment such as any board game like Go or Chess. I hope that one day they may be applicable to addressing social infrastructure challenges such as food production in underdeveloped communities.
